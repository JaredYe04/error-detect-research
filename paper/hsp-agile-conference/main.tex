\begin{abstract}
Ordered guard specifications require first-match precedence; LLMs and
test-feedback repair miss thin overlap regions.
We present \textsc{SgDP}/HSP-Agile with SpecIR regions, typed Semantic Feedback
IR (\textbf{+7.7\,pp} over unstructured test-only under~M on \texttt{ecnu-plus},
E6; E14: \texttt{semantic\_ir} 75.1\% vs.\ \texttt{execution\_trace\_matched}
85.4\%; \texttt{ecnu-max}/n1n show headroom-dependent $\Delta$),
and conjunctive release (E2: M PDR 95.0\%\,/\,FAR 5.0\% vs.\ B2 91.2\%\,/\,8.8\%;
bootstrap CI excludes 0).
\textbf{Deploy B2 by default;} use full~M when conjunctive $\mathrm{Accept}$
or prevention (PDR/FAR) is required.
On industrial-pattern SOFL ($n{=}31$), B2 reaches 100\% Conf.\ under gpt-4o and
Claude.
Canonical E1 (seed~0): B1 89.2\%, B2 87.7\%, M 86.3\% (all Holm n.s.; Strict
5.0\%; 46.2\,s vs.\ 13.9\,s).
E12-full (\texttt{run\_e12\_canonical\_v1}): B2 leads every seed (mean 88.0\%
vs.\ M 86.0\%; 4/120 wins; Friedman $p{=}0.077$).
\end{abstract}
