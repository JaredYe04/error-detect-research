\begin{abstract}
Ordered guard specifications require first-match precedence; LLMs and test-feedback
repair miss thin overlap regions.
We present \textsc{SgDP}/HSP-Agile with SpecIR regions, typed Semantic Feedback IR
(\textbf{+7.7\,pp} over test-only under controlled comparison, E6), and conjunctive
release.
\textbf{Deploy B2 by default;} use full M when conjunctive $\mathrm{Accept}$ or
prevention (PDR/FAR) is required.
Canonical E1 (seed~0): B1 89.2\%, B2 87.7\%, M 86.3\% (all n.s.).
E12-full (\texttt{run\_e12\_canonical\_v1}): B2 leads every seed (mean 88.0\% vs.\ M 86.0\%;
Friedman $p{=}0.077$).
\end{abstract}
