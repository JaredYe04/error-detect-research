\chapter*{Abstract}



\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Abstract}







Ordered guard specifications say which scenario must fire first when several



guards hold.

Large language models rarely obey that rule on thin overlap regions that

formal contracts pin down; unit tests almost never sample those regions, so

test-based repair leaves ordering defects in place.







We present \textbf{deployment-aware specification-guided repair} via the

\textsc{SgDP} framework, instantiated as \textbf{HSP-Agile} for SOFL/FSF.

Three mechanisms address that gap: \emph{SpecIR} semantic regions (first-match

partitions), a typed \emph{Semantic Feedback IR} for repair, and a

\emph{conjunctive} release gate (full witness conformance $\wedge$ no

high-severity structural pattern).







\textbf{Lead empirical claim (C2):} under identical repair budget $K{=}3$,

full Semantic Feedback IR improves mean formal conformance by

\textbf{+7.7\,pp} vs.\ unstructured test-only on \texttt{ecnu-plus}

(E6, $n{=}120$; paired wins/losses/ties 14/4/102; bootstrap 95\% CI

$[2.3,\,13.6]$\,pp; Wilcoxon $p{=}0.018$).

The claim is scoped to unstructured test-only---not uniqueness vs.\ every

structured trace (E14: \texttt{semantic\_ir} 75.1\% $\neq$

\texttt{execution\_trace\_matched} 85.4\%; paired 5/19/96).

On \texttt{ecnu-max} the same E6 protocol saturates (${\approx}89.2\%$ A/B/C),

so typed-IR gains are headroom-dependent.







\textbf{Prevention (C3):} conjunctive $\mathrm{Accept}$ improves prevention:

E2 impl-screening ($n{=}852$) gives mode~M PDR 95.0\% / FAR 5.0\% vs.\ B2

91.2\% / 8.8\%; paired bootstrap 95\% CIs on the M--B2 gaps exclude zero.







\textbf{Deployment boundary (C4):} prefer B2 by default when mean Conf.\ /

latency suffice; escalate to full mode~M \emph{iff} release requires

conjunctive $\mathrm{Accept}{=}1$ or target FAR${\le}5\%$---not from overlap

tertiles alone (canonical E3 is non-monotone).

On a curated industrial-pattern corpus ($n{=}31$), B2 reaches 100\% Conf.\

under gpt-4o and Claude while B1/M sit at 95.5\%.







As a near-ceiling stress-test under strengthened mode~M ($K{=}5$ multi-factor

\emph{bundle}, not equal-$K$ C2) and the fixed first-match others-witness

oracle, E1 M 100.0\% vs.\ B2 98.3\% (${+}1.7$\,pp; wins 2/120); E12 B2

100.0\% / M 99.7\% (B2 first every seed)---do not claim M dominates all seeds.

Under equal budget $K{=}3$ (\texttt{run\_e1\_equal\_k\_v1}), \texttt{M\_eq}

reaches 100.0\% vs.\ B2 97.5\% (${+}2.5$\,pp; wins 3/120)---Conf.\ ranking

hygiene, still secondary to E6.

A prior others-witness bug is disclosed as a \emph{measurement correction}.

Tasks, logs, and scripts are released.

Specification-guided repair; ordered guard specifications; LLM synthesis; SOFL;
