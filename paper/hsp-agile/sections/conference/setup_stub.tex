\textbf{Benchmark.} 120 hard synthetic tasks (\texttt{benchmarks/tasks.json},
overlap-filtered stress-test, E1) plus 100 uniform random tasks (E10), 22-task
external SOFL corpus (E11), and HumanEval/MBPP adapters (E8c).

\textbf{Modes.} B1 one-shot; B2 test-feedback ($K{=}3$); M full \textsc{SgDP};
ablations A1--A3 remove checker, pattern guard, or repair loop.

\textbf{Metrics.} Strict formal conformance (primary), strict success under
conjunctive Accept, mutation kill rate, latency, prevention PDR/FAR (E2).

\textbf{Protocol.} Canonical runs \texttt{run\_e1\_canonical\_v1} and
\texttt{run\_e12\_canonical\_v1} share benchmark fingerprint
\texttt{322157816b37c34e}; E16-canonical replicates 120 tasks with
\texttt{ecnu-max} (all modes 89.2\%, no ranking reversal).

\textbf{Power.} Post-hoc analysis shows $n{=}120$ is under-powered for M vs.\ B2
($\delta{=}{-}0.014$, observed power ${\approx}3.6\%$); we foreground E6 and
deployment evidence over aggregate ranking.

\textbf{Threats.} Overlap concentration (mitigated by E10/E8c); single primary
model (E16); synthetic benchmark bias (scoped claims).
