\textbf{Benchmark.}
BENCH-120: synthetic hard FSF tasks (5 inputs, 8 ordered scenarios) retained by
a Z3 overlap filter; E10 removes the filter ($n{=}100$) as a negative control.
Primary LLM: \texttt{ecnu-plus} (Qwen-family); secondary endpoints in the
cross-model subsection.

\textbf{Modes.}
B1 one-shot; B2 test-feedback ($K{=}3$); M full loop.
E6 holds mode~M fixed and sweeps feedback content at $K{=}3$.
Fixed-oracle stress-tests use strengthened~M ($K{=}5$ bundle); Conf.\ ranking
claims that require equal~$K$ use \texttt{M\_eq} ($K{=}3$).

\textbf{Metrics.}
Mean formal Conf.; Strict / $\mathrm{Accept}$; PDR/FAR on mutants; latency and
LLM calls.
Threats preview: synthetic construction, LLM non-determinism
($T_\text{repair}{=}0$), model headroom dependence.
