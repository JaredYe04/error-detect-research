\textbf{Benchmark.}
BENCH-120 is a synthetic \emph{hard} corpus (\texttt{benchmarks/tasks.json},
overlap-filtered stress-test; fingerprint \texttt{322157816b37c34e}).
Mitigations: E10 (100 uniform random tasks), E11 (22-task external SOFL
corpus), E8c (HumanEval/MBPP FSF adapters).

\textbf{Modes (main text).}
B1 one-shot; B2 test-feedback ($K{=}3$); M full \textsc{SgDP};
A1 no formal checker; A3 no repair loop.
B3--B5 (Self-Refine / Self-Debug / Reflexion-lite) and B6 (VerifierLoop-FSF)
appear in the supplementary material; A2 (no pattern guard) is cited for
ablation deltas only.

\textbf{Model.}
Qwen2.5-27B-Instruct~\citep{yang2024qwen25} ($T_{\mathrm{gen}}{=}0.7$,
$T_{\mathrm{repair}}{=}0$).
E16-canonical replicates 120 tasks with \texttt{ecnu-max} (all modes 89.2\%;
no ranking reversal).

\textbf{Metrics.}
Mean formal conformance (Conf.); Strict success under conjunctive
$\mathrm{Accept}$; prevention PDR/FAR (E2); wall-clock latency.

\textbf{Statistics.}
Paired Wilcoxon tests with Holm correction on per-task metrics; Friedman
test across E12 seeds for ranking stability.
Canonical runs: \texttt{run\_e1\_canonical\_v1},
\texttt{run\_e12\_canonical\_v1}.

\textbf{Power.}
Post-hoc: $n{=}120$ is under-powered for M vs.\ B2 aggregate Conf.\
($|\delta|{=}0.014$, observed power ${\approx}3.6\%$); we foreground E6
mechanism evidence and the deployment boundary over aggregate ranking.
