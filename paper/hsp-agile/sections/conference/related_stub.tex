Closest LLM+verifier systems differ on whether witnesses respect first-match
order, whether repair carries scenario structure, and whether prevention
(PDR/FAR) is measured~\cite{loughman2024dafnybench,sun2024clover}.
DafnyBench and Clover target proof-oriented oracles; Self-Refine, Self-Debug,
and Reflexion raise generation success via critique or verbal
memory~\cite{madaan2023selfrefine,chen2023selfdebug,shinn2023reflexion} but do
not emit region-typed failure records.
CodeT and coding agents (OpenHands, SWE-agent) optimise repository
tasks~\cite{chen2022codet,wang2024opendevin,yang2024sweagent} without an FSF
oracle $g_t$.
\textsc{SgDP} combines first-match SMT witnesses, typed Semantic Feedback IR,
and conjunctive $\mathrm{Accept}$ under PDR/FAR---not a claim that no prior
system uses any of LLM synthesis, CEGIS, or SMT checks.
We evaluate verbal/trace loops as B3--B5 and verifier-loop FSF as B6.
