\chapter{Conclusion and Future Work}
\label{ch:conclusion}

The industrial-monitoring contract \code{classify\_signal} asked a practical
question: can first-match ordering defects be prevented by using the
specification itself as evidence, without full deductive verification?
Three mechanisms and a deployment rule answer that question on overlap-intensive
specifications; \textsc{SgDP} / HSP-Agile is how they are realised.


%=============================================================================
\section{Summary}
\label{sec:conc:summary}
%=============================================================================

\paragraph{Problem.}
Test-based repair does not know which input regions the specification treats
as critical.

\paragraph{Four insights.}
(1)~Prevention should depend on semantic regions, not surface syntax---SpecIR
is the engineering hinge (E3 tertiles are non-monotone; no monotone-overlap
claim).
(2)~\textbf{Lead:} repair needs a typed scenario failure (Semantic Feedback IR),
not raw test bits (+7.7\,pp vs.\ test-only under controlled E6; paired
14/4/102; 95\% CI $[2.3,\,13.6]$; $p{=}0.018$; not vs.\ every structured
trace---see E14).
(3)~Release is conjunctive: full witness conformance \emph{and} a clear
high-severity pattern screen (E2/E17).
(4)~Prefer B2 by default; escalate to~M \emph{iff} $\mathrm{Accept}{=}1$ or
FAR${\le}5\%$ (Table~\ref{tab:deployment-boundary}).

\paragraph{Evidence.}
Lead: E6 typed IR ${+}7.7$\,pp vs.\ test-only on \texttt{ecnu-plus} (CI
excludes zero; E14 scopes C2: \texttt{semantic\_ir} 75.1\% $\neq$
\texttt{execution\_trace\_matched} 85.4\%); E2 prevention PDR 95.0\% /
FAR 5.0\% vs.\ B2 (bootstrap CIs exclude zero); C4 deploy B2 by default
unless conjunctive $\mathrm{Accept}$ or FAR${\le}5\%$ is required.
Secondary near-ceiling stress-test under fixed oracle / strengthened~$K{=}5$
\emph{bundle}: E1 M ${+}1.7$\,pp (2/120 wins); E12 B2 first every seed---do
not claim M dominates all seeds.
Equal-$K$ Conf.\ hygiene (\texttt{run\_e1\_equal\_k\_v1}): \texttt{M\_eq}
100.0\% vs.\ B2 97.5\% (${+}2.5$\,pp; 3/120 wins).
Adapters transfer to Mini-Z and Mini-StateMachine (E8).

\paragraph{Limitation.}
Fixed-oracle E1/E10/E12 sit near ceiling under strengthened~$K{=}5$; they are
stress-tests, not the primary effectiveness claim.
A prior others-witness bug had made Strict look uniformly ${\approx}5\%$---a
measurement artefact, disclosed as a correction enabling fair evaluation.
Residual failure taxonomy (E9) and pre-fix latency premiums ($3.3\times$)
describe older corpora; E8c, E10, and E12 bound external validity;
E16 and E6 on \texttt{ecnu-max} show saturation (ties /
${\approx}89.2\%$ A/B/C) rather than ranking reversal---typed-IR gains remain
primary-endpoint evidence under repair headroom.

\section{What to Remember}
\label{sec:conc:remember}
%=============================================================================

If only four facts survive:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item SpecIR matters because the prevention loop must be notation-agnostic
        while preserving first-match regions.
  \item Semantic Feedback IR matters because typed scenario structure improves
        repair over unstructured test dumps under controlled E6 (+7.7\,pp;
        E14 scopes the claim against length-matched traces).
  \item The acceptance gate matters because high mean conformance is not
        release---witnesses and patterns must both clear.
  \item Deployment follows release requirements: B2 default for mean conformance
        and latency; M when conjunctive $\mathrm{Accept}$ or PDR/FAR matter
        (Table~\ref{tab:deployment-boundary}).
\end{enumerate}


%=============================================================================
\section{Future Research Directions}
\label{sec:conc:future}
%=============================================================================

\paragraph{Richer encodings.}
Extend beyond linear integer guards to temporal properties, stateful
behaviours, and non-linear arithmetic---targeting ArithmeticError and
StateError residues in E9.

\paragraph{Stronger backends.}
Augment the finite witness suite with theorem-prover certificates for
candidates that already clear $\mathrm{Accept}$.

\paragraph{Adaptive budgets.}
Replace fixed $K=3$ with early stopping on easy tasks and extra iterations on
dense-overlap ordering failures.

\paragraph{Industrial replication.}
Apply HSP-Agile to practitioner SOFL/FSF specifications and measure prevention
against project acceptance criteria.


%=============================================================================
\section{Closing Remarks}
\label{sec:conc:closing}
%=============================================================================

\textsc{SgDP} is not a substitute for full formal verification.
It is a practical bridge built on three ideas---shared semantic regions,
typed repair feedback, and conjunctive acceptance---that keep many
specification-conformance defects out of release on overlap-intensive ordered
guard specifications while remaining usable inside a sprint.
The evidence that matters for deployment is E6 (+7.7\,pp), E2 (PDR/FAR), and
Table~\ref{tab:deployment-boundary}; fixed-oracle E1/E10/E12 are near-ceiling
stress-tests (M ${+}1.7$\,pp / ${+}1.0$\,pp aggregate; E12 B2 slightly more
seed-stable)---not a universal superiority claim across industrial corpora.
How far that bridge extends depends on richer encodings, industrial tasks,
and adaptive repair.
